• Before participating, you should register yourselves with the competition using your order reference number in the ticket. (http://codeblooded.mic.lk)
  • Competition will end at 1.30 a.m. on 18th May 2017.
  • Your registration information will be tracked by the organising committee.
  • You have to start from 1st challenge and continue in the order that has given in CCC challenges.
  • You will be able to see your current score through the CCC screen (right top corner).
  • If you skip a challenge, only up to the challenge prior to that (last completed challenge from beginning) will be counted when selecting the winner of the competition.
  • Whoever completes most number of the challenges before the end time (1:30 a.m. 18th May 2017) will win the competition.
  • If their are multiple candidates for the winners position, candidate who completed all the challenges first (took shortest time to complete) will be declared as the winner.
  • If you are lucky enough to be the winner, you have to provide valid registration information to verify yourself. If not, next highest scorer will be declared as the winner.
  • In case of a technical difficulty, you can seek support via the chat provided in the CCC website or from the people at help desk.
  • Decision of the organizing committee will be the final decision in any conflict resolution.
